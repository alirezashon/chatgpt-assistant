ChatGPT Workspace Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-10

Current local-first behavior

ChatGPT Workspace stores folders, assignments, favorites, preferences, onboarding state, and local backup data in Chrome extension storage on this browser. The current beta does not require an account, does not send workspace data to a backend, and does not connect to an external AI provider.

What is stored locally

  • Workspace folders and conversation assignments.
  • Extension preferences such as sidebar width and dismissed onboarding cards.
  • Favorites and local metadata needed to render the sidebar.
  • Backups only when you explicitly export them.

What is not collected by the current beta

  • No analytics events are sent from the extension.
  • No crash reports or local diagnostic reports are sent automatically.
  • No conversation content is uploaded to a ChatGPT Workspace server.
  • No external AI provider receives content from the extension.

Diagnostics

The extension can keep a small, bounded local history of extension errors in Chrome storage for troubleshooting. Users can export or clear diagnostics from Settings. Diagnostic bundles are generated locally and should be reviewed before sharing.

Future paid features

Paid features such as account sync, hosted AI processing, billing, and priority support will require an explicit opt-in privacy review before release. Free local workspace features will remain available without sign-in.
